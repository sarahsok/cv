Sarah Sok

Applied Linguist, Lecturer, and Assistant Director

I am an applied linguist with a specialization in second language acquisition. My research interests lie at the intersection of second language acquisition, computer assisted language learning, and language testing. I am currently a Lecturer and Assistant Director of Graduate Curriculum in the Program in Global Languages & Communication at UC Irvine, where I teach courses in academic writing and oral communication.
